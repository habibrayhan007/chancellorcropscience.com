About Us

Our Story

Chancellor Crop Science. Established in 2015,This is a fast growing professional Agrochemicals and Agriculture Development Company in Bangladesh ,Dealing with Fertilizers, Micronutrients, Plant growth regulators , Pesticides, and Aquaculture products.

Chancellor Crop Science follows international standards to ensure consistent quality of products and services to achieve customer satisfaction.

We follows international standards to ensure consistent quality of products and services to achieve customer satisfaction.

Chancellor Crop Science also meets all national regulatory requirements relating to its current business. This company has nine branch offices in different parts of this country.

This company imports Fertilizers, pesticides from USA, Turkey, Thailand, China, Germany, India, Malaysia and etc, as bulk and repack them in small packing in factory and make them available to the farmers Developments through own distribution network all over the country.

In the areas of health care and agriculture. With our innovative products, we are contributing to finding solutions to some of the major challenges of our time.
